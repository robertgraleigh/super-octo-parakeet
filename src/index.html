How We Work

Discover

First, we’ll start with a phone call to get to know you and your business better. ] Then you’ll be asked to fill out our new website questionnaire in an attempt to learn more details about you, your business, your marketing goals, and your industry.

Align

Then, we’ll use all of the information we’ve learned to design a website that strongly represents your brand and solves your marketing goals. Once we complete your design, we send you a live prototype of your website to review and leave comments.

Develop

Once the design is approved, we’ll then start developing your brand new website. This part is boring, so we’ll save you the details.

Ship

As we wrap up the website development, we’ll start uploading your content into the website, integrating security and analytics, and designing your new social media identity.

Repeat

30 short days later, we’ll be ready to launch your new website. It’ll be a good day, and we should celebrate!
